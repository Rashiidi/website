About

Hawaii is a group of volcanic islands located in the central Pacific Ocean, approximately 2,000 miles west of California. It is the only U.S. state completely made up of islands. The state has a rich history, with Polynesians settling in Hawaii around the 7th century. The islands were united under a single kingdom in 1810 by King Kamehameha I. In 1893, a group of sugar and pineapple businesspeople supported by the U.S. government overthrew the Hawaiian monarchy and Queen Liliuokalani [5]. The islands were formally annexed into the United States in 1898 and became a U.S. territory in 1900. Hawaii was admitted to the Union as the 50th state in 1959. .
